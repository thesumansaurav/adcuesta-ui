Introduction

Welcome to Adcuesta! We are a dynamic and forward-thinking company that specializes in both branding and mobile value-added services (mVAS). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with us.

Information We Collect

When you use our services or interact with our website, we may collect the following types of information:

  • 1) Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, phone number, and other contact details that you provide when you sign up for our services or contact us.
  • 2) Usage Data: We collect information about how you use our website and services, such as the pages you visit, the features you use, and the time you spend on our site.
  • 3) Technical Data: This includes your IP address, browser type, device information, and other technical details that help us improve our services and ensure they work properly.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • 1) To Provide Services: We use your personal information to deliver the services you request, such as branding and mVAS solutions.
  • 2) To Improve Our Services: Your usage and technical data help us understand how our services are being used and how we can make them better.
  • 3) To Communicate with You: We may use your contact information to send you updates, respond to your inquiries, and provide customer support.
  • 4) For Security: We use your information to protect our website and services from fraud and abuse.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following situations:

  • 1) With Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate our business, such as hosting providers, payment processors, and marketing agencies. These providers are required to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.
  • 2) For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe it is necessary to protect our rights, comply with a legal obligation, or prevent fraud or abuse.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • 1) Access your Information: You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • 2) Update Your Information: You can ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • 3) Delete Your Information: You can request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain legal restrictions.
  • 4) Object to Processing: You can object to our processing of your personal information in certain circumstances.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@adcuesta.com

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small files that are stored on your device and help us remember your preferences and understand how you use our site. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We use various technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, please be aware that no security measure is perfect, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
